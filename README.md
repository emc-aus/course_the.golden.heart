# course_the.golden.heart

You pray, fast, give zakat, and fulfil your obligations, yet still feel disconnected.
Maybe you feel stuck, unsure how to move forward. Maybe something feels like its missing.
Is your heart truly at ease with your relationship with Allah SWT? Do you know where you stand with Him?

Presented by Sheikh Salah Hamid, this series explores the diseases of the heart, their signs, and their cures.

Take the step to purify your heart and remove the spiritual obstacles standing between you and Allah SWT and His pleasure:
“Gardens of perpetual residence beneath which rivers flow, wherein they abide eternally. And that is the reward of one who purifies himself.” (Surah Taha: 76) 🍃
